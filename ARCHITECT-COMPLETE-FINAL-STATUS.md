## üí° KEY INSIGHTS (From Architecture Work)

**The Matching Algorithm is Everything**

- 70%+ show-up is the competitive moat
- Everything else (UI, payments, scaling) is supporting infrastructure
- Algorithm must improve weekly based on feedback
- By Month 5, this should be demonstrably better than VolunteerMatch

**Hyper-Local is Defensible**

- Don't compete nationally against VolunteerMatch
- Own neighborhoods at 20%+ nonprofit penetration
- Then expand city-by-city with proven playbook
- Geographic boundaries = moat that grows stronger with data

**B2B First Breaks Chicken-and-Egg**

- Nonprofits have acute pain + willingness to pay
- They recruit volunteers (you don't need to)
- Volunteers follow nonprofits (network effects)
- Revenue funds growth (no freemium burn)

**Data Loop = Continuous Improvement**

- Show-up % ‚Üí Feedback ‚Üí Algorithm iteration
- Week 1: 65% show-up ‚Üí analyze feedback ‚Üí adjust weights
- Week 2: 68% show-up ‚Üí iterate ‚Üí 70%
- Week 3: 72% show-up ‚Üí sustained advantage
